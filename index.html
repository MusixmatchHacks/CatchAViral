Aim: Track official music releases on youtube channels of musicians, labels, etc to add their lyrics first and boost the seo.


Info:
Irrelevant channel button: Paste the channel link and use this button to add more channels to be tracked for music releases. The channel link is the link of the youtube channel like: 'https://www.youtube.com/user/MIAVEVO'. The artistId can be found from the musixmatch admin panel but its not mandatory.
Flag channel button: Removes those channels which have irrelevant videos (channels where no music releases will be made) or unpopular channels.
Flag video button: Removes irrelevant videos and logs them to help train a model for detecting whether video is a new music release or not.

The page(video list) gets updated once every hour (1330h, 1430h, etc). It currently tracks 24000+ youtube accounts but a lot of them are irrelevant so please use the 'flag channel' and 'track this channel' button generously. The videos displayed will become more relevant as this page gets used more.
